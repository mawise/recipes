# Peanut Butter Couscous for Backpacking

## Ingredients
 - 1/2 cup water
 - 1/3 cup (dry) Moroccan Couscous
 - 1 T Olive Oil
 - 2 T Peanut Butter
 - 1/2 cube bullion

## Directions
 - Boil Water
 - add oil, bullion, peanut butter and stir
 - remove from heat, quickly stir in couscous, cover and let sit 5 minutes
